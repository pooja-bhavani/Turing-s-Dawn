# 🎬 Demo video shot-list — Turing's Dawn

**Goal:** a tight **60–90 second** clip that sells *tension + the Bombe + the dawn payoff*. The video is the single biggest lever on your placement — a flat screen-grab loses to a punchy cut. Record with **sound ON** (your synth audio is a differentiator).

**Tools:** macOS screen record = `Cmd+Shift+5` (record a window/region). Trim in QuickTime, or assemble in iMovie/CapCut. Browser at a clean 16:9 window, no devtools, no bookmarks bar.

**Prep:** play once first to warm up so your live take is smooth. Mute notifications. Full-screen the game window.

## Tips that punch above their weight
- **Lead with the meter draining** (shot 2). The clock is your differentiator — show it in the first 15s.
- **Let the Bombe halt on camera.** Don't cut away mid-run; the satisfaction is the payoff.
- **Keep captions short** (≤6 words). Burned-in text reads even on muted autoplay in the dev.to feed.
- **End on the URL** so a judge can click straight through.
- If you can only nail one thing: **shot 5 (the Bombe)** + **shot 6 (the closing dark)**.
